How It Works

BUILDING A BRIGHTER FINANCIAL FUTURE & GOOD SUPPORT

1. APPLICATION

1. Click Apply on navigation bar.
2. Attach certified ID copy, recent payslip/3 months bank statement by clicking Send.
3. Include your name and surname in subject line and state the amount to be borrowed.
4. Decision will be sent via SMS.

2. LOAN APPROVAL

1. A credit check will be done to determine your loan eligibility.
2. There's a 1-hour wait for processing.
3. You'll receive approval notification if successful.
4. Funds are then deposited into your account.
